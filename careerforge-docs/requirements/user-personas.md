# User Personas

## Purpose

This document defines the primary users of CareerForge. Understanding each user's goals, challenges, and expectations helps ensure that the platform is designed to solve real-world recruitment problems.

---

# Persona 1 – Job Seeker

## Overview

A Job Seeker is an individual searching for employment opportunities that match their skills, qualifications, interests, and career goals.

### Goals

- Find relevant job opportunities.
- Apply for jobs efficiently.
- Build a professional profile.
- Showcase skills, education, and projects.
- Track application progress.
- Increase chances of getting hired.

### Challenges

- Too many irrelevant job listings.
- Difficulty finding jobs that match their qualifications.
- Repeatedly entering the same information for applications.
- Limited visibility into application status.
- Difficulty presenting practical skills beyond a traditional resume.

### Needs

- Easy job search and filtering.
- Professional profile management.
- Resume and portfolio support.
- Simple application process.
- Application tracking dashboard.

### Success Criteria

The Job Seeker successfully discovers suitable opportunities, applies with confidence, and manages the recruitment process from one platform.

---

# Persona 2 – Employer

## Overview

An Employer represents a company or organization looking to recruit qualified candidates for available positions.

### Goals

- Publish job opportunities.
- Reach suitable candidates.
- Review applications efficiently.
- Manage recruitment activities.
- Hire qualified employees.

### Challenges

- Receiving many unqualified applications.
- Time-consuming candidate screening.
- Managing multiple job postings.
- Tracking applicants through different recruitment stages.

### Needs

- Company profile management.
- Job posting tools.
- Applicant management dashboard.
- Candidate search and filtering.
- Recruitment workflow management.

### Success Criteria

The Employer quickly identifies qualified candidates and completes recruitment with less time and effort.

---

# Persona 3 – Administrator

## Overview

The Administrator manages the CareerForge platform and ensures the system operates securely, efficiently, and fairly.

### Goals

- Maintain platform integrity.
- Monitor system activities.
- Manage users and companies.
- Handle reports and policy violations.
- Ensure platform reliability.

### Challenges

- Managing large numbers of users.
- Detecting fraudulent accounts or job postings.
- Maintaining platform quality.
- Responding to user reports.

### Needs

- Administrative dashboard.
- User management tools.
- Company management.
- Job moderation.
- Analytics and reporting.

### Success Criteria

The Administrator maintains a secure, reliable, and well-managed platform for all users.

---

# Summary

CareerForge serves three primary user groups:

| User | Primary Objective |
|-------|-------------------|
| Job Seeker | Find suitable employment opportunities and manage job applications. |
| Employer | Recruit qualified candidates efficiently. |
| Administrator | Maintain platform quality, security, and operations. |

Design decisions throughout the project should prioritize solving the needs and challenges identified for these user groups.
