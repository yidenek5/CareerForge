# Problem Statement

## 1. Background

The recruitment process has changed significantly with the growth of online job platforms. Every day, thousands of job opportunities are published across different websites, making it easier for employers to advertise vacancies and for candidates to search for employment.

Despite these advancements, the hiring process remains challenging for both job seekers and employers. Many existing platforms focus primarily on publishing job listings rather than improving the overall recruitment experience.

---

## 2. Problem Description

### Problems Faced by Job Seekers

Job seekers often experience several challenges throughout the recruitment process.

- Finding relevant job opportunities among thousands of listings can be difficult.
- Many recommended jobs do not match their skills, qualifications, or career goals.
- Applying for multiple jobs requires repeatedly submitting the same information.
- Candidates often have no simple way to organize and track the progress of their applications.
- Traditional resumes do not always provide a complete picture of a candidate's practical skills, projects, and achievements.

As a result, job seekers spend significant time searching for opportunities without knowing whether they are applying to the right positions.

---

### Problems Faced by Employers

Employers also face challenges during recruitment.

- Job postings often receive a large number of applications from candidates who do not meet the required qualifications.
- Reviewing applications manually consumes considerable time and effort.
- It is difficult to quickly identify candidates whose skills closely match the job requirements.
- Managing job postings, applications, and recruitment activities across different tools can become inefficient.

These challenges slow down the hiring process and increase recruitment costs.

---

## 3. Impact

The limitations of current recruitment systems affect both individuals and organizations.

For job seekers:

- Increased time spent searching for suitable jobs.
- Lower confidence in finding opportunities that match their qualifications.
- Difficulty managing multiple job applications.

For employers:

- Longer recruitment cycles.
- Increased workload for recruiters.
- Greater difficulty identifying qualified candidates.

---

## 4. Proposed Solution

CareerForge is designed to improve the job discovery and recruitment process by providing a centralized platform where job seekers and employers can interact more efficiently.

The platform enables job seekers to create professional profiles, search and filter job opportunities, apply online, and monitor their application progress.

Employers can create company profiles, publish job opportunities, review applicants, and manage recruitment activities through a single system.

By organizing recruitment into one platform, CareerForge aims to reduce unnecessary complexity, improve communication between candidates and employers, and support more efficient hiring decisions.

---

## 5. Expected Outcome

CareerForge aims to create a recruitment platform that is:

- Simple to use.
- Efficient for both job seekers and employers.
- Organized and transparent.
- Scalable for future enhancements.
- Focused on improving the quality of job matching and recruitment management.

The long-term goal is to make the recruitment process faster, more organized, and more effective for everyone involved.
